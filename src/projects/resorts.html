The hotel room showcase, built with modern tech

The brief

In my urge for summer during the long swedish winter, I wanted to build something beautiful that reminded me of warmer days. This project consisted of creating a site for featuring hotel rooms.

The goal was to give the images as much space as possible, and give an easy feeling to the website. Through clear and consitent design patterns and images that speak for themselves, the user experience is pleasant, and gives me something to look at on grayer days.
